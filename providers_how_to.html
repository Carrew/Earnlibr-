🏢 How Providers Work on EarnLibr

Step 1: Click “Create Offer” from your dashboard.
Step 2: Fill in your offer title and full details clearly.
Step 3: Upload a clear image for your offer.
Step 4: Set your promotion budget (amount you are willing to spend).
Step 5: Set commission carefully — ONLY what you can afford to pay per verified deal.
Step 6: Submit your offer for approval/activation.
Step 7: Track performance inside “My Offers”.
Step 8: Manage deals and responses from promoters.
Step 9: Pay commissions only for verified completed deals.
Step 10: Improve offers based on performance results.
🔥 IMPORTANT: Join the Provider WhatsApp/Messenger groups for support, updates, and faster deal approvals.
