4. Offers & Deal Flow

4.1 Offers

Providers create offers with a full description, price, commission rules, and a promotion budget. Offers may be set to:

  • Pending – awaiting admin review.
  • Active – available for promoters to share.
  • Paused – temporarily disabled.
  • Closed – ended or fully used budget.
  • Flagged – under review for possible violations.

4.2 Deal Completion & Cancellation

A deal is considered Completed when the client has requested the offer, the provider has delivered the service or product,marks the deal as completed and the system has verified this, potentially including client confirmation.

If a provider marks a deal as Cancelled, the system will first verify the cancellation using:

  • Activity logs and timestamps.
  • In-app chat history between client and provider.
  • Any additional internal verification signals.

A deal is not treated as cancelled until EarnLibr confirms the cancellation after this verification, to protect promoters from unfair deal removal.

5.2 Promoter Earnings

All payouts are processed by Admin and may depend on provider settlement and internal verification. We reserve the right to adjust or reverse payouts resulting from fraudulent or invalid activity.

Promoters earnings are calculated after deduction of the platform's percentage on each commission.

The platform holds the right to update the platform"s percentage on each commission at anytime which takes effect on all activities after the update, all promoters will be notifed.
