This Cookie Policy explains how EarnLibr (“we”, “our”, “us”) uses cookies and similar technologies when you use our website or platform.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your actions and preferences over time, such as login state, language, and usage patterns.

2. How We Use Cookies

2.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are required for the platform to work properly. They are used to:

  • Keep you logged in and maintain secure sessions.
  • Help prevent fraud and protect your account.
  • Enable basic platform features.

2.2 Performance & Analytics Cookies

We use these cookies to:

  • Understand how users navigate the platform.
  • Identify which pages or features are most used.
  • Measure SmartLink performance (clicks, conversions, time).
  • Improve platform speed, reliability, and overall experience.

2.3 Functional Cookies

These help us remember your preferences, such as:

  • Language settings.
  • Interface choices or layout preferences.

2.4 Limited Tracking for Referrals

EarnLibr uses tracking parameters in SmartLinks to attribute referrals and conversions to the correct promoters. This is a core function of the platform.

We do not use ad-network style tracking cookies to follow you across unrelated websites, and we do not sell your usage data to advertisers.

3. What Data Cookies May Collect

Depending on the cookie, we may collect:

  • Session identifiers and login state.
  • Device and browser information.
  • IP address (for security and fraud detection).
  • Pages visited and actions taken on the platform.
  • Referrer and SmartLink tracking codes.

Cookies do not store your password or direct payment information.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use limited third-party services (for hosting, analytics, or payments). These providers may set their own cookies in order to deliver their services. They are required to handle data securely and use it only for their specific function within EarnLibr.

5. Managing Cookies

Most browsers allow you to manage cookies through their settings. You can:

  • Block all cookies.
  • Delete existing cookies.
  • Allow cookies from selected sites only.

However, blocking essential cookies will prevent EarnLibr from functioning correctly, including login, tracking deals, and using dashboards.

6. Consent

By using the platform, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy. Where required by law, you may see a banner or prompt asking you to accept cookies when you first visit.

7. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.

8. Contact

If you have questions about our use of cookies, contact us at teamt@earnlibr.com.
